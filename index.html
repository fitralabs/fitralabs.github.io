Sustainable Sadaqah

We build for the Ummah, sustained by the community. For voluntary sadaqah and donation contributions, net proceeds are split equally between charity and the ongoing care of our tools.

Care (50%)

Half of net donation proceeds helps cover engineering, hosting, design, accessibility, support, and the long-term maintenance of each product.

Charity (50%)

The other half supports selected charitable causes, including humanitarian relief, education, and community support.
