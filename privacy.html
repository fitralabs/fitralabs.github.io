Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Fitra Labs ("we," "us," or "our") builds essential tools for the modern Muslim. Our philosophy is rooted in "Fitra" - the natural state of purity and simplicity. This policy explains how we handle data in our mobile applications (including Simply Quran and Simply Qibla) and on our website, fitralabs.app.

1. Purity of Data

We believe that your spiritual journey is private. Unlike many modern applications, Fitra Labs tools are designed to function without requiring you to create an account, provide an email address, or link your social media profiles.

2. Local Data (Your Device)

The core content of our apps is stored and processed locally on your device.

  • Reading Habits: We do not track which Surahs or Ayahs you read.
  • Bookmarks: Your bookmarks and "Last Read" positions are stored in a local database on your phone and never leave your device.
  • Location: For tools like Simply Qibla, your GPS coordinates are used only for mathematical calculations performed on-device. We do not store or transmit your location history.

3. Technical Integrity (Crash Reporting)

To ensure our apps remain stable and "Simply" to use across thousands of different device models, we utilize Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google.

  • What is collected: If the app crashes or encounters a major error, an anonymous technical report is sent to us. This includes technical info such as your device model, operating system version, and a "stack trace" of the code at the time of the error.
  • What is NOT collected: These reports do not contain your name, your identity, or any of your private app data (like bookmarks).
  • Retention: Technical diagnostic data is automatically deleted from Google's servers within 90 days. We use this information solely for debugging and performance optimization.

4. No Third-Party Tracking or Ads

  • No Ads: We will never include third-party advertisements in our tools.
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties.
  • No Analytics: We do not use behavioral analytics to track how you use the app or to build a profile of your habits.

5. Compliance with Malaysian Law (PDPA)

In accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010, we inform you that we do not process personal data for marketing purposes. Because our tools are designed for anonymity, we do not possess "Personal Data" as defined by the Act that can identify you as an individual.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our tools or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please reach out to us at salam@fitralabs.app.
