Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Fitra Labs (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to the Simply Quran mobile application and our website, fitralabs.app.

1. No Data Collection

Simply Quran is designed with a “Privacy-by-Default” philosophy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or location information.

  • Local Storage: All app data (bookmarks, last-read positions) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
  • No Third Parties: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud tracking.

2. PDPA Compliance (Malaysia)

In accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010, we provide this notice to inform you that no personal data is processed by Fitra Labs through our minimalist tools.

(Notis Privasi: Selaras dengan Akta Perlindungan Data Peribadi 2010, kami memaklumkan bahawa tiada data peribadi dikumpul atau diproses oleh Fitra Labs melalui aplikasi kami.)

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at salam@fitralabs.app.
